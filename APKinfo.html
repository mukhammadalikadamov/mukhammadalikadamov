How to download APK files on your device


Once when you click on "OK", it will start downloading the file. Once when its done, you go to your files and click on the APK file.


it may show up this if you haven't got to settings and turn on allow unknown apps, to ignore, click on "Settings".


You will have to click on yes or it won't allow you to download the APK, when your done, click on the Arrow.

When you click the Arrow, it will take you back to the download, you click on yes, and you installed your APK app.
